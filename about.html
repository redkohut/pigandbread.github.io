Who We Are?

Welcome to our bakery! We are passionate about creating delicious baked goods, from sourdough bread to delicate pastries and stunning cakes. Located in the heart of Kyiv, Ukraine, we take pride in using the finest ingredients and traditional techniques to produce high-quality, flavorful treats that our customers love. At our bakery, we believe that the key to great baking lies in the quality of the ingredients and the care with which they are prepared. That's why we make our own sourdough starter and use it to create a range of artisan breads, each with its own unique flavor and texture. We also offer a wide variety of sweet treats, from classic French pastries to decadent cakes and tarts. But we don't just make delicious baked goods - we also love to share our knowledge with others. That's why we offer classes on bread baking and sourdough starter creation, so that you can learn to make your own artisanal breads at home. Whether you're a seasoned baker or a novice, our classes are a fun and informative way to improve your skills and expand your culinary horizons. So come visit us at our bakery in Kyiv and discover the joys of great baking for yourself!
